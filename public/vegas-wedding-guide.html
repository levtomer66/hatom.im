Pro Tips for Israeli Couples

This is extremely common

Thousands of Israeli couples marry in Las Vegas every year. Every office has handled Israeli couples before.

Get the license the day BEFORE the ceremony

Valid for a year. Getting it a day early means zero stress on your wedding day.

Order extra certified copies (3-4 minimum)

They're cheap ($10-15 each) and you need them for apostille + Israeli registration + spares.

Get the apostille before leaving the US

Nevada Secretary of State Las Vegas office (555 E. Washington Ave). Same-day in person saves weeks.

Bring $102 cash for the license

Credit cards work but add a surcharge ($105.29). Cash = exact amount, no fees.

Choose a chapel with limo/concierge service

Several chapels offer hotel → bureau → chapel transport. Makes the whole experience seamless.

Watch for hidden surcharges

A Little White Chapel and Viva Las Vegas add 19% surcharge. Most chapels charge +$60 officiant fee on top. Always ask for the all-in price.

Total timeline after return: 4-8 weeks

Apostille → translation → registration → new Teudat Zehut. If relaxed, 2-3 months.
